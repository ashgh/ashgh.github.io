About Us


Play for Equality

is a game stream with one goal, to raise awareness and help organizations involved in the effort to dismantle racist ideologies present in today's society. We will be live streaming ourselves playing games, discussing topics about inequality, systemic racism, approproation, and representation. We will also talk about various organizations that we have featured in our link section.


If you want to help out these organizations, click on the "Donate" link above or check out the links below.
