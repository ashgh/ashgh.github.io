Playing for Equality: Project Justice is a 24-hour game stream marathon with one goal, to raise awareness and help organizations invovled in the effort to dismantle racist idealogies present in today's society. We will be live streaming starting at July 11th 2020 8AM PST. If you want to help out, you have a couple options. If you dontate to us directly, we will work with Amazon to match all donations made before July 6th. If you don't want to dontate to us, we have provided many links below to vetted organizations broken up by category. Anything helps!
